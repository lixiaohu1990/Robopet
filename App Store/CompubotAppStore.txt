App Name: 

	Compubot

App Categories:

	Primary: Games
	Secondary: Entertainment

App Keywords:

	game, robot, computer, pet

App Description:

	Help your cute new robot pet, Compubot stay happy, healthy and energized by playing 3 fun mini games! 
	Have you always wanted to own a robot? If yes, Compubot is the pet for you! 
	This 100% free game consists of three fun mini games to keep you glued to your screen:

	•The roll game: to increase Compubot’s power level, collect batteries before it runs out of juice while avoiding bumpers. 
	•The music game: to increase Compubot’s happiness level, click L.E.D lights on Compubot’s controller to complete a song. 
	•The jump game: to increase Compubot’s wellness, level avoid viruses and boxes to keep the robot healthy and virus free.

	Each mini game can be accessed through Compubot’s room which is the home screen.
	Compubot is waiting for you!
